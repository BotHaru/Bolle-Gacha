Kamisato Ayaka

Overview

High burst damage Cryo sword user. Best in Freeze and Mono-Cryo teams, with strong energy generation and flexible playstyle.

Talents & Abilities

  • Normal Attack: 5-hit combo.
  • Elemental Skill: AoE Cryo DMG, launches enemies.
  • Elemental Burst: Large Cryo storm with high multipliers.
  • Alternate Sprint: Infuses Cryo after dash.
  • Talent Priority: Burst > Skill > Normal Attack.

Artifacts

  • 4‑pc Blizzard Strayer — best for Freeze comps.
  • 2‑pc Blizzard Strayer + 2‑pc Noblesse Oblige — extra Burst DMG.

Weapons

  • Best-in-Slot: Mistsplitter Reforged, Haran Geppaku Futsu.
  • Budget: Amenoma Kageuchi — F2P option with ER sustain.

Teams & Rotation Tips

  • Freeze teams with Hydro applicators and Anemo support.
  • Mono-Cryo with Shenhe, Rosaria, Diona.
  • Rotation: Support buffs → Ayaka Sprint → Skill → Burst.
