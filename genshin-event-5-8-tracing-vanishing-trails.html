Tracing Vanishing Trails

Patch: v5.8 • Dates: Aug 14 → Aug 25, 2025

Overview

Exploration-focused event featuring route tracking. Follow clues and waypoints to locate hidden objectives and collect rewards.

Phases & Mechanics

  • Route Quests: Complete exploration tasks and puzzle nodes along designated routes.
  • Timed Trials: Optional time trials along paths for extra currency and challenge.
  • Completion Checks: Map prompts indicate remaining objectives per area.

Event Currency

  • Exploration Tokens
  • Route Seals

Use currency at the Event Shop to redeem rewards before the event ends.

Rewards

  • Primogems
  • Mora & Ores
  • Furnishings / Gadgets (if applicable)

Tips & Team Suggestions

  • Use Anemo/Geo resonance or movement gadgets to traverse faster.
  • Mark chests and puzzles on the map to avoid backtracking.
  • Swap to puzzle-solvers (e.g., Anemo for vents) when needed.
