Patch: v5.8 • Dates: Aug 16 → Sep 1, 2025

Overview

Special TCG ruleset where certain actions are automated or enhanced. Win matches against AI decks to obtain currency and cards.

Phases & Mechanics

  • Special Rules: Automated plays or free actions after specific triggers; check rules per round.
  • Challenge Decks: Fight themed AI decks with unique mechanics and objectives.
  • Score & Progress: Earn points from wins; higher difficulties grant more currency.

Event Currency

  • TCG Tokens

Use currency at the Event Shop to redeem rewards before the event ends.

Rewards

  • Primogems
  • Card Backs / Cosmetics

Tips & Team Suggestions

  • Use decks with consistent elemental reactions and energy generation.
  • Adapt to the round's special rule (e.g., extra dice, free skill).
  • Focus on tempo; mulligan aggressively for your core combo pieces.
