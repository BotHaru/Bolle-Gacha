Albedo

Talents & Abilities

  • Elemental Skill (Abiogenesis: Solar Isotoma): Creates a Geo construct that generates Transient Blossoms (AoE Geo DMG based on DEF) every 2s when enemies are in range. Snapshots buffs at cast time.
  • Elemental Burst (Rite of Progeniture: Tectonic Tide): Unleashes Geo crystals; if Solar Isotoma exists, spawns 7 Fatal Blossoms. Burst scales with ATK, blossoms snapshot buffs.
  • Passives:
    • A1: Blossoms deal +25% DMG to enemies under 50% HP.
    • A4: Burst increases party's EM by 125 for 10s.
    • Utility: 10% chance to double weapon ascension materials.
  • Talent Priority: Skill > Burst > Normal Attack.

Artifacts

  • 4‑pc Husk of Opulent Dreams — top choice for DEF-scaling skill DPS.
  • 4‑pc Golden Troupe — alternative, slightly lower DPS.

Weapons

  • Best-in-Slot: Cinnabar Spindle, Uraku Misugiri.
  • Budget: Harbinger of Dawn — strong 3★ option if HP >90%.

Teams & Rotation Tips

  • Excels in Double Geo comps (e.g., Zhongli, Itto, Noelle).
  • Rotation: Gorou E → Albedo E → Yun Jin E → Noelle E/Burst, Zhongli shield for comfort.
